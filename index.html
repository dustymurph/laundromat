About us

Koo Wee Rup Laundromat is your friendly neighbourhood self-service laundromat. Here, we're all about making your laundry dry a breeze.

We've got super-clean, top-notch, state-of-the-art machines in various sizes - perfect for washing, drying, or both. We also have large-sized dedicated pet washers and dryers for your furry friends. Plus, we have lots of parking lots in our spacious car park behind our laundromat.

Experience the Koo Wee Rup Laundromat difference today and see why our regulars love us!
